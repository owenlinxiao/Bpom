About HACCP Certification

HACCP, or Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points, is a preventive food safety management system. It ensures food safety and hygiene by analyzing hazards in food production, processing, storage and transportation, identifying critical control points, and implementing control measures.

The HACCP global certification provided by our institution is internationally recognized, helping enterprises enhance market competitiveness and meet the access requirements of domestic and international markets.
